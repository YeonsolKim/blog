#### Adjectival Modifier
Meanings.
1. subjective
2. objective
3. genitive
4. time, place, reason, manner

Examples. 
1. The girl who is playing in the garden is my sister.
2. He is the man whom I met yesterday.
   He is the friend to whom i gave the present. 
3. A man whose wife is dead is called a widower.
4. The day when he left was Monday.
