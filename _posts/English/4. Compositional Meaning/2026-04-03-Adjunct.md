## Circumstance
> This originated in Melanesia: 공간적 배경
> This emerged during the war: 상황적 배경(가끔 시간적 배경을 의미할 수도)

## Manner(과정의 양태)
> He solved the problem with great difficulty: 그는 많은 우여곡절 끝에 문제를 풀었다.
> He spoke politely.

## Purpose
> She studied hard to pass the exam


## Result
> The boy grew up to be a famous singer.

## Stimuli
>I'm pleased to see you again.
>She smiled to see such a beautiful sight.

## Evidence
>He must be crazy to behave like that.

## Condition
> To hear them talk, you would think they are old friends.

## 화자의 평가
> Naturally, he was very angry.

## Level of Generality 
> Broadly speaking, there are two ways to fix the problem.

## Origin
### He was born of a wealthy family.
* Subject: he
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(VP): born of a wealthy family.
    * Head: born
    * Complement(PP): of a wealthy family.
      * Head(P): of
      * Complement: a wealthy family

### That house is made of brick.
