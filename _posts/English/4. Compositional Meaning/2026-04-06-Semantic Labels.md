### 1. Action
1. Agent(의지를 가지고 동작을 수행하는 주체), Theme
2. Agent, Patient(단순 대상이 아니라 내부적으로 상태가 변화하는 대상)
3. Agent, Proposition

> Examples.
> 1. The company exported rice to Japan.
> 2. He ate the apple.
   She painted the wall.
> 3. The teacher recommended that she be sent home.

### 2. Experience
Experiencer(의지적 작용이 아니라 심리적, 감각적 인지를 경험하는 주체), Experience(경험 내용)

>Examples.<br>
>I feel cold.<br>
   She loves music.<br>
   I believe she will come home.

### 3. Attribution
1. Object, Function
2. Object, State

>Examples.
>1. This key opens the back door.
>2. I was hospitalized.<br>
   English is spoken in many countries.<br>
   The data is being processed.<br>
   The road leads to the city center.

### 4. Deontic
Rule, Content

>Examples.<br>
>The law forbids smoking in this area.

### 5. Causation
1. Cause, Physical Effect
2. Cause, Perceptual Effect

>Examples. 
>1. The cold froze the river.
>2. The music makes me happy.<br>
>   The simple interface disguises the system’s complexity.

### 6. Indication
Evidence, Indication

>Examples.<br>
>The study suggests that caffeine improves memory.

### 7. Conceptual
1. Term, Definition
2. Term, Classification
3. Term, Property

>Examples.
>1. A square is a rectangle with four sides of equal length.
>2. Mammalia belongs to the phylum Chordata.
>3. Mercury has a very high surface tension.

### 8. Establishing The Conditions
Enabler(event가 성립할 수 있는 상황적 조건을 마련하는 요소), Event
>Examples.<br>
>This technology allows users to share files instantly.<br>
>The road gives access to the village.


### 9. Formation
Source of Formation, Formed Result
>Examples.<br>
>The environment I grew up in made me who I am today. <br>
>The river formed these deposits. 

### 10. 
Event, Driving(진행)
>The mountain walk took us from Kreuth to Lake Achen.<br>
>That incident drove me to this state. (그 사건은 나를 이 지경으로 몰고갔어.)
