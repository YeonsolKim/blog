### 1. Verb Interpretation
**I was invited.**
* Raised Subject: I
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): invited
    * Predicand: I
    * Predicate: invited


> Simplification: (I) was / ~ invited.


### 2. Adjectival Interpretation 
**Her phone was broken.**
* Subject: Her phone
* Predicate
  * Head(Lexical Verb): was
  * Complement(AdjP): broken
