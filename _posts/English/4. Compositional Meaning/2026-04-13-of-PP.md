adjunct로 기능하는 of-PP의 grammatical meaning은 relational marker 정도이다. 문장들 내에서는 다양한 compositional meaning을 나타낸다. 
### 1. Attribute 
>a man of courage


### 2. Carrier 
>the color of the sky


### 3. Origin
>a man of noble birth


### 4. The whole to which that part belongs 
>the handle of this spot (physical whole)<br>
>skeletons of whales (anatomical whole)<br>
>the innermost depths of the human soul (spatial whole; metaphor)


### 5. Content 
>the problem of the low birth rate<br>
>the way of life <br>
>the world of individual emotions (domain)


### 6. Possessor 
>the assets of a nation


### 7. Cause
>the shock of the news

### 8. Substance 
>a wall of stone


### 9. Producer
>the works of Shakespeare


### 10. Apposition
>the city of Rome


### 11. Relative
> the brother of John
