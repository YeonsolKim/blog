adjunct로 기능하는 of-PP의 grammatical meaning은 relational marker 정도이지만 문장 내에서의 compositional meaning은 다양하다. 
### 1. attribute 
>a man of courage<br>
>the color of the sky


### 2. origin
>a man of noble birth


### 3. part - whole 
>the handle of this spot (physical part)<br>
>skeletons of whales (anatomical part)


### 4. content 
>the problem of the low birth rate


### 5. possession 
>the assets of a nation


### 6. result - cause
>the shock of the news

### 7. substance 
>a wall of stone


### 8. product - producer
>the works of Shakespeare


### 9. apposition
>the city of Rome
