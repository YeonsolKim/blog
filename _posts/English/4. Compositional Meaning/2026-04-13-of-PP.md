adjunct로 기능하는 of-PP의 grammatical meaning은 relational marker 정도이지만 문장 내에서의 compositional meaning은 다양하다. 
1. attribute (e.g., a man of courage, the color of the sky)
2. origin (e.g, a man of noble birth)
3. part - whole (e.g., the handle of this spot(physical part), skeletons of whales(anatomical part))
4. content (e.g., the problem of the low birth rate)
5. possession (e.g., the assets of a nation)
6. result - cause (e.g., the shock of the news)
7. substance (e.g., a wall of stone)
8. product - producer (e.g., the works of Shakespeare)
9. apposition (e.g., the city of Rome)
