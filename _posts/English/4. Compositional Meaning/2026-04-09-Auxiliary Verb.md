# 1. can
1. 능력
2. 허락
3. **가능성**
4. **추측**

>Examples.
>1. He can lift 500N.
>2. You can go home now.
>3. Even expert drivers can make mistakes.
>4. She can't be tired now. 

# 2. may
1. 허락
2. 추측

# 3. must, have to
1. 의무
2. **필요**
3. 강한 추측

>Examples. 
>1. You must do military service.
>2. I have to call my mom.
>3. He must be hungry.

# 4. will
1. **의지**
2. **cooperativeness**
3. **disposition(경향)**
4. **습성(습관적)**
5. **확실한 추측**

>Examples. 
>1. I will lose weight.
>2. The door won't open.
>3. After sad things, happy things will happen. (슬픈 일 뒤에는 행복한일이 생기기 마련이다.)
>4. I would sometimes travel alone. (나는 때로 혼자 여행을 하곤 했다.)
>5. He will be a doctor.

# 5. should, ought to
1. 의무
2. **합리적 추측**
