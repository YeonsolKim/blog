### 1. Modifier in NP
1. Subject
>the rise of the sun


2. Object
> the construction of the dam<br>
> the conquest of the world



3. Attribute 
>a man of courage


4. Carrier 
>the color of the sky


5. Origin
>a man of noble birth


6. The whole to which that part belongs 
>the handle of this spot (physical whole)<br>
>skeletons of whales (anatomical whole)<br>
>the innermost depths of the human soul (spatial whole; metaphor)


7. Content 
>the problem of the low birth rate<br>
>the way of life <br>
>the world of individual emotions (domain)


8. Possessor 
>the assets of a nation


9. Cause
>the shock of the news

10. Substance 
>a wall of stone


11. Producer
>the works of Shakespeare


12. Apposition
>the city of Rome


13. Relative
> the brother of John

### 2. Modifier in VP
1. Origin
   >He was born of a wealthy family.
   
2. Ingredient
>That house is made of brick.
