## 1. Structures
1. (predicand) (intransitive verb)
2. (predicand) (intransitive verb) (predicative complement) 
3. (predicand) (transitive verb) (complement)
4. (predicand) (transitive verb) (direct object) (complement)
5. (predicand) (transitive verb) (clause complement)

>Examples.
>1. (Physics) has been treading.
>2. (Discrepancies that might appear at the cosmological level) are (extremely difficult to probe experimentally).
>3. (Lord Kevin) talked (about two clouds).
>4. (He) congratulated (her) (on her promotion).<br>
>   (He) persuaded (her) (to change the tactics).
>5. (He) expected (her to change the tactics).

## 2. Predicate types
1. event-related predicates
2. static predicates: state, relation, etc.

## 3. Semantic roles
느슨하게 object 등으로 처리할 수도 있지만, 그게 가능하지 않은 경우에 semantic roles는 유용하다.
1. agent (의도적으로 행위를 수행)
2. cause
3. patient (내부적 변화를 겪는 대상)
4. theme
5. experiencer
6. stimulus (감정·지각·인식을 유발하는 대상)
7. instrument
8. location
9. source
10. goal 
11. recipient
12. beneficiary
