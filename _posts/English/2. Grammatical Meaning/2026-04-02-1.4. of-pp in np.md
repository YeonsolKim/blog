### 1. Complement
**three of the boys**
* Head(N): three
* Complement(PP): of the boys
  * Head(P): of
  * Complement: the boys

### 2. Subject Corresponding Complement
#### the rise of the sun
* Determiner(D): the
* Head(N): rise
* Subject Corresponding Complement(PP): of the sun
  * Head: of
  * Complement: the sun

### 3. Object Corresponding Complement
of 뒤에 overt NP가 오는 전형적 of-PP에서는, 그 NP가 object로 기능하는 것이 거의 대부분이다. 
> the construction of the dam<br>
> the conquest of the world
