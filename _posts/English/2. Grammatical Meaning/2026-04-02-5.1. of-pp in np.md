### 1. Complement
**three of the boys**
* Head(N): three
* Complement(PP): of the boys
  * Head(P): of
  * Complement: the boys

### 2. Subject Corresponding Complement
#### the rise of the sun
* Determiner(D): the
* Head(N): rise
* Subject Corresponding Complement(PP): of the sun
  * Head: of
  * Complement: the sun

### 3. Object Corresponding Complement
of 뒤에 overt NP가 오는 전형적 of-PP에서는, 그 NP가 object로 기능하는 것이 거의 대부분이다. 
#### the construction of the dam

### 4. Adjunct
grammatical meaning: relational marker
1. attribute (e.g., a man of courage, the color of the sky)
2. origin (e.g, a man of noble birth)
3. part - whole (e.g., the handle of this spot)
4. content (e.g., the problem of the low birth rate)
5. possession (e.g., the assets of a nation)
6. result - cause (e.g., the shock of the news)
7. substance (e.g., a wall of stone)
8. product - producer (e.g., the works of Shakespeare)
9. apposition (e.g., the city of Rome)
