## 2. Complement
대체로 verb와의 파생 관계(derivational relation)를 반영한다. 따라서 대부분의 경우 object corresponding이다. 
### 2.1. Object Corresponding Complement
#### the construction of the dam
#### abandonment of his ship
#### criticism of her decision
#### abandonment of his ship

### 2.2. Subject Corresponding Complement
#### the rise of the sun
* Determiner(D): the
* Head(N): rise
* Subject Corresponding Complement(PP): of the sun
  * Head: of
  * Complement: the sun
