이 경우 be는 modal auxiliaries이므로 transparent verb이고, 따라서 raising이 일어난 구조이다. 
#### I was invited.
* Raised Subject: I
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): invited
    * Predicand: I
    * Predicate: invited


> Simplification: (I) was / ~ invited.
