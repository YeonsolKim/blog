of 뒤에 overt NP가 정상적으로 오는 전형적 of-PP에서는, 그 NP가 object로 기능하는 것이 거의 대부분이다. 
### 1. Complement
**three of the boys**
* Head(N): three
* Complement(PP): of the boys
  * Head(P): of
  * Complement: the boys

### 2. Subject Corresponding Complement
**the rise of the sun**
* Determiner(D): the
* Head(N): rise
* Subject Corresponding Complement(PP): of the sun
  * Head: of
  * Complement: the sun

### 3. Object Corresponding Complement
**the construction of the dam**

### 4. Adjunct
**a man of courage**

**a man of noble birth**

**the handle of this spot**

**the problem of the low birth rate**
