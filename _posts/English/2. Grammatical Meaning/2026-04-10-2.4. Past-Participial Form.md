### 1. Verb Interpretation (be-passive)
사건적 해석. 
이 경우 past-participial infinitival clause인 경우이다. 
noun의 post modifier로 오는 past participle 구조는 기본적으로 past-participial clause로 본다.

#### I was invited.
* Raised Subject: I
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): invited
    * Predicand: I
    * Predicate: invited


> Simplification: (I) was / ~ invited.


### 2. Adjective Interpretation (adjectival passive)
상태적 해석. 
이 경우 adjectival phrase이다. 

#### Her phone was broken.
* Subject: Her phone
* Predicate
  * Head(Lexical Verb): was
  * Complement(AdjP): broken


> Simplification: Her phone / was / broken.
