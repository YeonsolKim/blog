## Auxiliary Verb의 Complement로 기능하는 Past-Participial Clause
#### I was invited.
이 경우 be는 modal auxiliaries이므로 transparent verb이고, 따라서 raising이 일어난 구조로 볼 수 있다.  
* Raised Subject: I
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): invited
    * Predicand: I
    * Predicate: invited


> Simplification: (I) was / ~ invited.



#### It must have been in the spring of 1920.
* Subject: It
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): must
  * Complement(Bare Infinitival Clause): have been in the spring of 1920
    * Predicate: have been in the spring of 1920
      * Head(Auxiliary Verb): have
      * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): been in the spring of 1920
        * Predicand: it
        * Predicate: been in the spring of 1920
          * Head(Lexical Verb): been 
          * Complement(PP): in the spring of 1920


>Simplification: (It) / must / have [it been in the spring of 1920].



## Modifier로 기능하는 Past-Participial Clause
#### rocks formed from river deposits
* NP
  * Head: rocks
  * Modifier(Past-Participial Clause): formed from river deposits
     * Predicand: rocks
     * Predicate: formed from river deposits
