### She made the boys stay after school.
* Predicate: 
  * Head: made
  * Complement(Bare Infinitival Clause): (the boys) stay after school
    * Predicand: the boys
    * Head: stay
    * Adjunct(PP): after school

### I didn't see you come in.
* Predicate: 
  * Head: didn't
  * Complement(VP): see you come in
    * Head: see
    * Complement(Bare Infinitival Clause): (you) come in
      * Predicand: you
      * Predicate: come in
