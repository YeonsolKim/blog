clause의 VP가 그 subject에 해당하는 constituent에 의미역을 주지 않을 때 raising이 일어났다고 보고, 그 constituent를 raised subject라고 한다. appear, seem, begin 같은 일부 lexical verb와 대부분의 auxiliary verb는 transparent verb이다. 

### He seems to be incorrect.
* Raised Subject(NP): He
  * Head(N): He
* Predicate(VP): seems to be incorrect
  * Head(Lexical Verb): seems
  * Complement(to-infinitival Clause): to be incorrect
    * Predicand: he
    * Predicate(VP): be incorrect
      * Head(Auxiliary Verb): be
      * Complement(AdjP): incorrect
        * Head(Predicative Adj): incorrect


> Simplification: seems / he to be incorrect
