### 1. Passive
사건적 수동. 즉 past-participial infinitival clause인 경우이다. 
한편, 이 경우 be는 modal auxiliaries이므로 transparent verb이고, 따라서 raising이 일어난 구조이다. 
#### I was invited.
* Raised Subject: I
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): invited
    * Predicand: I
    * Predicate: invited


> Simplification: (I) was / ~ invited.


### 2. Adjectival
상태(사건으로 해석될 여지도 있다). 
이 경우 adjectival phrase이다. 
#### Her phone was broken.
* Subject: Her phone
* Predicate
  * Head(Lexical Verb): was
  * Complement(AdjP): broken


> Simplification: Her phone / was / broken.
