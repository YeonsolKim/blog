**Definition of limit.** 

$$\lim_{x\to a}f(x)=L\Longleftrightarrow \forall \varepsilon >0\ \exists \delta >0\ \forall x\,\left(x\in D\wedge 0<|x-a|<\ \delta \rightarrow |f(x)-L|<\varepsilon\right)$$

$$\forall f\ \forall a\ \forall L\ \left( \lim_{x\to a} f(x) = L \iff \forall \varepsilon > 0 \ \exists \delta > 0 \ \forall x \in D \ \left(0 < |x-a| < \delta \to |f(x)-L| < \varepsilon\right) \right)$$
