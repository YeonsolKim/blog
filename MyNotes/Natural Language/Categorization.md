### Lexical Categories
1. Verb
2. Noun
3. Determinative
4. Adjective
5. Adverb
6. Preposition
7. Subordinator
8. Coordinator
9. Interjection
(For the first six of the nine lexical categories, there are phrasal categories.)

### Sentence Components
1. Verb
2. Subject
3. Object
4. Complement
5. Modifier(Adjectival/Adverbial)

### Clauses
A clause is a special type of phrase: one that has a VP as its head. 
The shortest possible clause is like "Run!".
