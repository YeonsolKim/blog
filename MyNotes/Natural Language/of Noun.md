#### 1. Adjectival Modifier
Meanings in NP. 
1. partitive (부분) 
2. subjective 
3. objective 
4. appositive (동격) 
5. ablative (기원)
6. 성질

Examples. 
1. the handle [of this pot], three [of the boys]
2. the rise [of the sun]
3. the construction [of the dam]
4. the problem [of the low birth rate]
5. a man [of noble birth]
6. a man [of courage]

#### 2. Adverbial Modifier
Meanings in VP.
1. 기원
2. 재료
3. 이유
4. 대상

Examples.
1. He [was born [of a wealthy family]].
2. That house [is made [of brick]].
3. He [died [of a heart attack]].
4. I [heard [of the news]].
