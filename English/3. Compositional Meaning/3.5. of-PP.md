# 2. Subject Corresponding Complement
### the rise of the sun
꾸미는게 아니라 확실히 complement네. 
* Determiner(D): the
* Head(N): rise
* Subject Corresponding Complement(PP): of the sun
  * Head: of
  * Object: the sun
